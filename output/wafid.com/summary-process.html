Accreditation Process Summary

Prepare All Required Documents

You'll need the following documents in your Medical Center Accreditation Application:

  1. A stamped Authorization Letter of the medical center representative as per GHC policy. Fill out a new Authorization Letter here.

  2. A copy of a valid medical center License.

  3. A copy of the medical center owner Criminal Record Certificate.

  4. A copy of the medical center owner Educational Qualifications.

Fill Out The GHC Medical Center Accreditation Application Form

Share with us all the medical center information needed to assess your application.Fill out your application here

Pay The Application Processing Fee

Finalize the processing fees payment of your GHC Medical Center Accreditation Application on time to confirm your application. You will receive the payment portal link of your application in your e-mail inbox after submitting your application.

Track Your Application Status

Use the Accreditation Application Status Inquiry Page to get the most recent updates on your application. Also keep an eye on your e-mail address inbox for any updates or inquires related to your application.
